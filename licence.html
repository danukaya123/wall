Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to credit the photographer or QUIZONTAL?

Attribution is not required but is always appreciated. If you choose to provide attribution, you can credit the photographer and/or QUIZONTAL. For example: "Photo by John Doe from QUIZONTAL"

Can I use QUIZONTAL content in client projects?

Yes, you can use our content in client projects without any additional licensing requirements. The Free Content License covers commercial use, including work for clients.

Are there any restrictions on the number of downloads?

No, there are no limits on how many images or videos you can download from QUIZONTAL. You can use our platform as much as you need for your projects.

What about recognizable people or private property in photos?

Our contributors are responsible for obtaining necessary releases for recognizable people and private property. However, we recommend exercising caution when using such content for commercial purposes.

Can I redistribute QUIZONTAL content on my own website?

You can redistribute unmodified content as long as it's not in a competing service. If you're creating a collection or gallery, we recommend linking back to the original content on QUIZONTAL.
